Steps to Make a Cup of Tea

  1. Boil water in a kettle.
  2. Place a tea bag or tea leaves in a teacup.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the tea bag or tea leaves.
  4. Let it steep for a few minutes.
  5. Remove the tea bag or strain the tea leaves.
  6. Add sugar, honey, or milk as desired.
  7. Stir and enjoy your tea!
