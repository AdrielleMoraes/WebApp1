Phases of the Moon

Chapter 1 : The New Moon

Once upon a time, the night sky was blanketed in darkness.

There was no moon to be seen. This was the beginning of the lunar cycle, the New Moon.

The world awaited the birth of a new celestial companion.

Chapter 2 : The First Quarter Moon

As the days passed, a crescent of light began to grace the sky.

It was the First Quarter Moon, a delicate half-moon, signaling the moon's awakening.

It divided the night between light and shadow, a harbinger of change.

Chapter 3 : The Full Moon

Time flowed like a river, and soon the moon had come full circle.

It was the Full Moon, a radiant sphere illuminating the night.

Its brilliance touched every corner of the Earth, filling the world with silver light.

Chapter 4 : The Third Quarter Moon

In the days that followed, the moon began to wane, showing less of itself each night.

It reached the Third Quarter, another half-moon, but this time it was fading.

The world prepared for the moon to bid adieu.
