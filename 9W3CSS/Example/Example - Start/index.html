Power-Up Mushroom

A Power-Up Mushroom, is a red mushroom with white spots that causes Small Mario to turn into his Super form, allowing him to smash through bricks and take an extra hit from enemies. Power-Up mushrooms were inspired by the concept of mushrooms being associated with magical worlds in general, like the wonderland of Alice's Adventures in Wonderland and the Mushroom Kingdom. Regardless, the item has become one of the most iconic items in video game history, and it is almost as known as Mario himself.
